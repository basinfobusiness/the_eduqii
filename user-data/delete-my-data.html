Delete my account data

This action permanently removes your profile, activity records, learning progress, and all stored personal data. It cannot be undone.

What will be deleted

  • ProfileAccount details, email, and settings.
  • ProgressQuiz attempts, scores, and saved activity.
  • PreferencesSaved learning preferences and theme choices.
  • SupportAny active subscriptions or support records linked to this account.
You must type DELETE and check the box before the button is enabled.

Need help?
