Exam Day Checklist

Before the exam:
- Confirm exam date, time and venue
- Pack ID / admit card / stationery (pens, pencil, eraser, ruler)
- Bring a fully charged calculator (if permitted) and spare batteries
- Prepare water and a light snack (check venue rules)
- Sleep well and eat a healthy meal before the exam
- Print any permitted reference materials (if allowed)

At the exam start:
- Arrive 15-30 minutes early
- Check seating and invigilator instructions
- Read the entire question paper quickly to plan time
- Note the marks for each question and allocate time accordingly

During the exam:
- Attempt easier questions first to secure marks
- Show clear workings and label answers where required
- Keep track of time; leave 10-15 minutes for review
- If unsure, flag the question and return to it later

After the exam:
- Review your answers if time permits
- Make note of topics to review for next time

Good luck!
